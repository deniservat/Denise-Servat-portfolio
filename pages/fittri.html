PROBLEM

How can we provide a new user with the best possible experience? Not only do we want them to use the app, but we want them to integrate it into their life as a daily tool and be assisted in meeting their fitness goals.

GOAL

Educate users on balanced&healthy eating.

Provide tools to achieve their fitness goals.

Motivate them to take action to be held accountable.

SOLUTION

Simple recipes, easy steps.

Define categories per taste.

Nutritional facts.

PROTO PERSONAS

They were validated with 5 interviews, which provided deeper insight of the users.

Alex spends her time mostly at work or school. As a student, she’s on a tight budget but still wishes to mantain a healthy lifestyle. She tends to squeeze in workouts in between study sessions and always opts for a healthy home cooked meal whenever she has time.

MOTIVATIONS

  • Clean eating.
  • Having emough time to herself.
  • Meeting fitness goals.

PAIN POINTS

  • Healthy options don’t taste as good.
  • Grocery shopping can be pricey.
  • Lack of free time.

CORE NEEDS

  • App needs to tell the nutrition facts.
  • Specific meals to her diet.
  • Gather inspiration for cooking.
